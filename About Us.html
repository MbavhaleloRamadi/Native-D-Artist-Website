Meet Native D

Native D, the lyrical phenomenon from the heart of Vhembe, making waves in the music world. Rooted in the vibrant culture of Venda, I am not your typical artist. With a knack for storytelling through rap, I am a creative force and wordsmith extraordinaire.

Growing up in the same neighborhood as the renowned audio engineer Kurtis Guap, My destiny was set. Our shared love for music ignited a lifelong passion and brotherhood.

My music is a fusion of English rap and Venda tradition, taking you on a lyrical journey through picturesque landscapes. My music, tell stories of self-discovery during my hiatus from the spotlight. They're more than just songs; they're a testament to my evolution and wisdom, from numerology to audio engineering.

Mission Statement

I am a fiercely independent recording artist/producer with exceptional abilities. I assist my art arrangement and producetion in developing musical art by producing, promoting and distributing my music and helping to build my brand so that I can free up time and resources to invest in my creativity.

I pride myself in maintaining close, cohesive relationships with my key executives, art and team.

Vision

Maintain a consistent level of mutual respect and attention to detail with my art. Understand the creative process. Guide my art, but also pay attention!! Actively involved in the music publishing and help to maximize income through proper collection efforts and placement of songs in all forms of media and advertising.

Goal

Not only develop the art, but to develop long, mutually rewarding relationships that are long term. True art development.
